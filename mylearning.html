My learning Plan

Long term goal:

My long-term goal is to develop my programming skills, think like a developer, and find my first job in the tech industry.



My strengths and limitations in learning:

I worked in customer focus roles over the years which improve my interpersonal skills and ability to work in a group. It also helped me to develop myself as a team player and improve my leadership skills. I am currently working policy environment where I need to adapt to new policy changes on very short notice and flexible while working in a high-pressure environment. My limitation is sometimes I try to be a perfectionist and spend more time than required.

Skills I would like to see developed:

I would like to improve my communication skill and behavioral-based interview technique which I believe is a very important step to land yourself in the job market.



How will I manage my workload:

Regular intervals during learning, listen to some music to clear my mind and come back with a fresh start. Follow timetable/time-box /toggle to track the time.



How will I seek help:

I am comfortable asking for help from fellow students & facilitators but at the same time, I first try to debug the issue by myself before reaching out for help.



Expectations of the facilitation team:

Learn new ideas, assist with issues if needed. Guidance throughout the learning process to understand what to expect from each learning sprint and each week of Bootcamp.



External commitments:

For the first five weeks of foundation, I’m working full-time, I have a strict timetable to follow and also I’m committed for study on weekends as well. Last ten weeks I’m taking a break from my full-time job, social circle, and fully dedicated to Bootcamp.
