Foundations Reflection

Core learning:

Three things I found helpful during core learning are emotional intelligence, growth mindset, and neuroplasticity. I learn how learning new things can shape your brain. How your thought process and attitude shape your mindset. Also the value of emotional intelligence in day-to-day life and how it helps to become a good human being and achieve success at a professional level.



Values, Empathy, and Self Awareness in Programming:

It's very important to be a team player as it helps you to fit in any team. Empathy, values, and self-awareness play a very important role when you deal with your daily workload, interact with your clients, colleagues, bosses or manage a team of people with different cultures and backgrounds. “Mahi tahi” is important as you share a common goal in a team.



Core Learning Surprises:

The neuroplasticity and Growth Mindset part of the core learning was very useful. I was grateful to learn this concept and it helps to keep me motivated and I'm confident that it will help me to achieve long-term goals.



Core Learning Challenges:

I think identity and values were the challenging part of core learning. Going into flashbacks and talking about choices, values, and upbringing was time-consuming for me. Also, I took a lot of time to summarise the information.



EDA and Core Learning:

Core learning is one of the important aspects to becoming a successful programmer as most of the tech companies look for tech skills plus attitude and interpersonal skills (team fit). On the other hand, I think I spend lots of time on core learning which could be utilized to clear javascript concepts and understand the logic to use those concepts. But at the end of the day, I feel both skills are equally important to grow as a successful developer.
