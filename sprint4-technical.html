Problem-solving techniques:

Pseudocode:

It helps you understand the problem by divide the problem into small stages which makes more sense in the code. It gives you a pathway to translate the code.

Rubber ducky method:

Repeating it back step by step helps me to figure out the issue during my learning process and I like this . It's like when we try to teach something to somebody eventually you also get better at it.

Googling:

Googling is the best as you can use keyword and check other relevant problems or someone else who come across the same problem as you. I use this technique frequently.

Reading error messages:

It helps you figure out the root cause of the problem and may lead to debugging the issue.
